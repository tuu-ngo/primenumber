How it works

  • A prime number is a natural number greater than 1 that has no positive divisors other than 1 and itself.
  • Uses trial division up to √n with 6k±1 optimisation — efficient for all safe integers.
  • Handles edge cases: 0, 1, negative numbers, decimals, and non-numeric input.
  • All computation runs entirely in your browser — no server required.
